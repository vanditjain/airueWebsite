  • ERP

    Clients today are increasing building robust internal systems that come with certain intrinsic attributes. These are systems that are meant to fit business needs, help optimize costs and are easy to maintain with the lowest possible total cost of ownership. The enterprise system is the nerve centre of the fabric that runs through the organization. ERP is one of the most accomplished practices within AIRUE INVO. We have added substantial value to clients through engagements that span from stand alone implementations, regional/ global roll outs, upgrades, migration and complex transformation programs that involve significant change management and technology implementation.
    Deploying the core comes with its own set of challenges and responsibilities that extend to an ecosystem residing within each of our client environments.Typically,once the core ERP system is successfully deployed, our teams extend the engagement to associated applications and integration. We have built unique workflows, industry specific modules all of which integrate to the core ERP application . These systems are built on object oriented and open standards, hence easy to integrate and maintain.
